VR the Future:

Letting anyone experience everything

Available in more places

Our VR Environments are compatible with Google Cardboard, Android, iOS, GearVR, Google Daydream, Playstation VR, Oculus Rift, HTC Vive, WebVR, and more . . .

No Downloading required

All of the VR Expiriences that we create are made through website URLs. This allows for you to be able to easily experience the Virtual Reality Environment that we have created.

Making VR Cheaper

Due to the fact that our Experiences are available on mobile devices, it allows for people across the planet to be able to view our experiences without having to buy an expensive, non-portable, desktop based VR experience.

We build for all industries

We are able to develop Virtual Reality Environments for almost every industry. This feature grants us with seemingly Unlimited Poential to be able expand into any industry and revolutionize the entire design.

Creating a fun place to work

Because we encourage our staff to create Virtual Reality Environments for many different industries, it helps us to create teams of individuals that are passionate about your industry and ultimately makes innovation, creative, and forward thinking products in the end.

Our Unique Design

We design entire worlds of digital architecture to immerse you into a world filled with purposeful fenetration to evoke a feeling of true presense in a space. Our designs are specifically tailored to you and your VR narrative. Notably, the design culture at TAU VR is very minimalistic, futuristic, subtle, and elegant.
